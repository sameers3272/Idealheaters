About Us

Established in 1998, by Mr.B.M.Shaikh, we-“Ideal Heaters” is one of the leading manufacturer and supplier of industrial heaters and heating elements.

We have very well backed by our highly skilled and committed workforce, who has helped us to attain maximum customer satisfaction. Our professionals are employed only after testing their skills in their respective domain. Our workforce consists of the following:

  • Technicians

  • Administrators

  • Engineers

  • Quality controllers

We have been manufacturing quality products are custom designed and manufactured as per specifications of our clients and providing them. Our huge assortment includes mica band hearers, ceramic band heaters, cartridge heaters, hi-watt density heaters, strip heaters, Water immersion heaters, oil immersion heaters, coil heaters, air heaters, air finned heaters, quartz heaters, infra red heaters, corrugated machine heaters, silica heaters, casting heaters, furnace heating elements, heating mental, muffle furnace, industrial ovens.

In addition to this, we also offer thermocouple sensors of various ranges of temperature and of various types like RTD PT-100, J-type (iron/constantan), K-type (chromal/alumel), R-type (platinum/platinum/rhodium13%), S-type (platinum/platinum/rhodium10%)
